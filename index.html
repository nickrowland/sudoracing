Rules

  1. Don't be a dick
  2. Race clean and give room
  3. Be polite and respect others
  4. BLUE FLAGS
    1. Race: blue flag = maintain predictable pace/line, don't defend against overlapping car
    2. Quali: blue flag on hotlap = maintain predictable pace/line, don't defend against overlapping car
    3. Quali: blue flag on non-hotlap = no sudden braking or movements, let overlapping car past if safe
    4. Pit Exit: cars leaving the pits have priority but must stay inside the exit line, cars on track must leave room for cars exiting the pit lane
  5. Enjoy yourself!
